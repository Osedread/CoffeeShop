About us

We are an independent coffee shop located in the heart of Bristol. We are specialised in bringing the best types of coffee around the world. Every month we will be serving ten different grounds coffees that you can taste and leave your opinions on Twitter. At the end of the year you will decide the best resource of coffee and we will bring it back for Christmas, joining it to the regular menu.

Come and help us choose!
